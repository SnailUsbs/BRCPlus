# BRCPlus
BRC Plus is a mod that aims to add various fun and QoL based features to bomb rush. Every feature in the mod can be controlled, toggled, and or used through the BRC Plus phone app

# Features:

## >FUN:
- **Turn into any object in your map, or the base game as whole:** You can use the phone app to turn into what ever prop you are closest to, or have it list out each of the props in your current map.

- **Change Player Size:** You can now change the player size to *"small"*, *"Big"*, *"Huge"*, and normal which is the default option.

- **Change The Gravity Level:** You can now use the phone to set the gravity level to normal, low, or high.

- **Always In Speed Zones:** You can now make the game you are always on one of those paths that give you a speed boost when you slide through them.

- **Spawn Something:** You can now spawn characters, vending machines, cars, and props directly in front of you, where ever you are, with an easy remove all button to get rid of them.


## >GAMEPLAY:
- **Normal, Quick, & Auto graffiti spray modes:** You can now switch the type of spray mode you do when spraying a graffiti from your phone, with quick just skipping the mini game, and auto just automatically doing the graffiti when you get in its hit box

- **Character & Move Style Switching:** You can now switch between base characters, character outfits, and move styles from the BRC Plus app, including random options for when you just can't pick out something/want to try something new.

- **Travel To Any Map Through Your Phone, Including The Police Station:** You can now go to any map in the base game, including the police station which you can noramlly only go in at the start of the game.

- **God Mode:** You can toggle god mode from your phone, which will ensure that your health can never drop 


## >MOVEMENT:
- **Ultra Goon:** You can now get full control over your goons, with an option to force set your stored speed, another option to lock your stored speed mid game, and one more option to toggle billboards to always make you goon.

- **Infinite Air Dash:** You can now enable Infinite Air Dashes.

- **Infinite Boost:** You can toggle your boost to be infinite from your phone

- **Control If Spraying A Graffiti Spot Gives You Boosts:** You can now block getting boosts added to your boost meter when you tag a graffiti spot


## >HEAT:
- **Disable All Heat:** You can disable all heat from your phone

- **Remove / Add Heat:** You can add and or remove heat from your heat level from your phone


## >UI Options:
- **Hide Any UI You Want:** You can toggle any UI to be hidden or visible through your phone


## >OPTIMIZE:
- **Hide Gaining Boost Animation:** You can now toggle the animation that plays on your screen whenever you gain boost

- **Hide All Graffiti:** You can now have all graffiti in a given map despawn through a 1 time check that happens as you are loading into that map. 


## >EXTRA:
- **Switch Save Files Without Going To The Title Screen:** You can now switch save files mid game through your phone

- **Cut Scene Tool:** Will let you play stage specific cut scenes whenever you want from your phone. (Stage must support the cut scene. i.e: the police cut scenes wont work in hideout)
